Who is Waiting?

In August 2017, 13,597 individuals were on the waiting list. Most were in the Critical category, indicating that services were needed within one year.

 

In August 2017, 18% of the individuals on the waiting list had caregivers over age 60 (n=2,422). Most of these were in the critical category (n=1,701). Percentages in the bar chart are based on the total number on the waiting list.
